Notable Research Projects

Optimizing the Lead Page of CRM Logic (2022)

As the capstone project for us three students at DePaul, we aimed to improve accessibility of CRM Logic, a pre existing CRM software. We carried out surveys, interviews, and chalkmark testing with low and mid-fi prototypes in order to construct data-informed UX solutions.

Report Document (PDF)

Kiosk Accessibility for Vision Impaired Users (2021)

During this course on designing for accessibility, our class had the opportunity to meet with a panel of blind guests. Each guest shared stories about their personal experiences with technology - analog and digital alike. Three classmates and I conducted private interviews with several panelists, resulting in our discussion and considerations found in this document.

Report Document (PDF)

Pretty Penny System Mock Research Report (2019)

With a group of four students of HCI at DePaul, we learned the process of basic user research. Our group conducted several interviews, created personas and activity concepts to extract new designs. Our job was to understand prospective users from our limited point of view.
