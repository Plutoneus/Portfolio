Notable Research Projects

Optimizing the Lead Page of CRM Logic (2022)

As the capstone project for us three students at DePaul, we aimed to improve findability of CRM Logic, a pre existing CRM software. We carried out surveys, interviews, and chalkmark testing with low and mid-fi prototypes in order to construct data-informed UX solutions.

Kiosk Accessibility for Vision Impaired Users (2021)

In 2021, our class had the opportunity to meet with a panel of blind guests. Each guest shared stories about their personal experiences with technology - analog and digital alike. Three classmates and I conducted private interviews with several panelists, resulting in our discussion and considerations found in this document.

Report Document (PDF)

Itch.io Web Accessibility Report (2021)

During a course on web & software accessibility, we conducted analyses on our choice of website. Itch.io allows users to upload their games and demos for public (or private) availability. Since I value the space that Itch.io provides for developers and gamers, I felt it should be analyzed for accessibility quirks. I list my concerns, tools, methods, and results within the attached PDF.
